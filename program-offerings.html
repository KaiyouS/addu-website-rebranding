Academic Programs

Ateneo de Davao University provides a wide array of undergraduate programs across its main academic units: the School of Arts and Sciences, the School of Business and Governance, the School of Engineering and Architecture, the School of Education, and the School of Nursing. The university also supports students preparing for further studies and careers in fields like medicine and law. On the postgraduate level, Ateneo de Davao offers numerous graduate programs, featuring Master's degrees across diverse disciplines and doctoral degrees such as the Doctor of Philosophy (in fields like Clinical Psychology and Development Studies), Doctor of Business Administration, Doctor of Public Administration, and Doctor of Education Administration.
